Request Account Deletion

Valet Waste (the "App" and web portal) · Last updated: July 7, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Valet Waste account and the personal data associated with it.

How to request deletion

  1. Email valetwastefl@allsynccrm.com from the email address on your account.
  2. Use the subject line "Account deletion request."
  3. Tell us the name and email (and, if you're a customer, the service address) on the account so we can locate it.

We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days, and confirm by email when it's complete.

What gets deleted

Your account profile, contact details (name, email, phone), service addresses, saved photos, location clock-in records, and any saved notification tokens are permanently deleted.

What we may keep

We may retain invoices, receipts, and payment records for the period required by tax and accounting law, and any information we must keep to comply with legal obligations. Card details are held by our payment processor, Stripe; we never store full card numbers.

Contact

Questions about deletion or your data: valetwastefl@allsynccrm.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
